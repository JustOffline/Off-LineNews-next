Facebook BANNED
🇨🇳 China · 🇮🇷 Iran · 🇰🇵 North Korea · 🇷🇺 Russia
One of the most broadly blocked platforms globally. China's firewall has blocked it since 2009. Russia designated Meta an extremist org in 2022. Myanmar blocked it in Feb 2021 following the military coup.

YouTube RESTRICTED
🇨🇳 China · 🇮🇷 Iran · 🇷🇺 Russia (throttled)

Telegram RESTRICTED
🇧🇷 Brazil · 🇪🇸 Spain (temp) · 🇮🇷 Iran
Brazil suspended Telegram in Aug 2024 over failure to remove neo-Nazi content. Spain's Supreme Court ordered a 5-day block in Mar 2024 amid copyright dispute. CEO Pavel Durov arrested in France in Aug 2024.
Last updated: June 2026
WhatsApp BANNED
🇨🇳 China · 🇰🇵 North Korea · 🇨🇺 Cuba (restricted)
Blocked as part of China's Great Firewall. North Korea restricts all foreign internet platforms. Cuba has faced repeated messaging app blocks during protest periods.
Last updated: June 2026
LinkedIn BANNED
🇷🇺 Russia · 🇨🇳 China
Russia blocked LinkedIn in 2016 for non-compliance with data localisation laws — the first major Western platform to be blocked there. China blocked it in 2023 after removing its social feed.
Last updated: June 2026
Twitch RESTRICTED
🇹🇷 Turkey · 🇨🇳 China · 🇰🇷 South Korea (partial)
Turkey blocked Twitch in Aug 2024 over refusal to appoint a local representative. South Korea restricted access to streams broadcasting illegal gambling content.
Last updated: June 2026
Bluesky MONITORING
🇧🇷 Brazil (briefly blocked 2024)
Brazil's Supreme Court ordered Bluesky blocked in Aug 2024 for failing to appoint a legal representative. Block lifted within days after compliance. First significant test of the platform's legal resilience.
Last updated: June 2026
Pinterest BANNED
🇨🇳 China · 🇮🇷 Iran · 🇰🇵 North Korea
Blocked as part of China's Great Firewall. Blocked in Iran since 2012. Access prohibited in North Korea as part of its broader internet blackout.
Last updated: June 2026
Discord RESTRICTED
🇨🇳 China · 🇷🇺 Russia · 🇹🇷 Turkey (periodic)
Blocked in China. Russia has issued repeated block orders against Discord, citing failure to remove content deemed illegal. Turkey has periodically throttled or blocked it during civil unrest.
